The seed prompt

“Generate using three.js a voxel-style scene of an F1 car driving around a 3D representation of [a real Grand Prix circuit] … with play / pause / restart and orbit controls.”

Shared controls across entries: Space play/pause · R restart · drag to orbit · scroll to zoom. No external models or textures — every track, car, and tree is generated in code.
