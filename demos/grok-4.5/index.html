How it was built

Prompts

Session prompts that shaped Trail Brakers with Grok 4.5 — from the seed voxel scene through Silverstone accuracy, barriers, the classic 3D sibling, and this showcase landing page.

  1. 01 Seed 00:53
    generate using three.js a voxel-style scene of an F1 car driving around a 3D representation of Silverstone circuit. The user should be able to play, pause, and restart the animation as well as rotate the map to look at the circuit and car from different angles.
  2. 02 Fix 00:56
    I hit play but nothing happened
  3. 03 Layout 00:59
    the basic functionality of the game work though it does not look like the actual silverstone circuit. Can you research and look up the real silverstone and model it accurately?
  4. 04 Scenery 01:04
    can you ensure the building and the grandstands are in their proper location? Also mark the corners with their names and T numbers?
  5. 05 Detail 01:09
    I think we should make the turn labels taller. Also the grandstands look like they're oriented the wrong way. Can you do some research on what the buildings look like and how we can model them accurately? Also there are some missing patches in the road that needs to be filled in?
  6. 06 Map 01:12
    can you expand the grass around the track? Some of the stands are going off map
  7. 07 Camera 01:14
    can you have a follow car button which will follow the car from a 3rd person perspective from behind?
  8. 08 Safety 01:17
    there are blue and white blocks around the track, are those supposed to be the walls? Some of them are on the track itself and seem to be randomly placed around the circuit. If they're supposed to be the track walls, can we research the runoff areas, gravel, and walls of silverstone and implement them accurately?
  9. 09 Barriers 01:23
    There are some issues with the placement of continuous perimeter walls, blue blocks only at TecPro zones at turns 3–5 (which are labeled or placed incorrectly as they appear to go T5–3 in descending order), Turn 6–7 have perimeter walls that don't seem to connect correctly, are on the track, and have random curves. Also on Maggotts and Becketts as well as T16 Vale. Can you check again by researching and consulting with the visual data of Silverstone to correct these mistakes?
  10. 10 Barriers 01:31
    That did not change anything, perhaps we should take a different approach and attempt to place all of the wall segments manually in their proper location as opposed to using math, because the distances from the walls and the track are not uniform. Some will be closer than others especially at the loop and how close it is to maggotts and becketts. So perhaps we should research the track's barrier locations throughout and then place the barriers in the corresponding locations
  11. 11 Cleanup 01:43
    I think we are nearly there with the barriers and the tec-pro, we need to verify that they all connect and that we don't have overlays like at T3, T6 on both sides, T9 has a slight gap in the barriers, Maggotts and Becketts towards Aintree and the Loop have barrier overlaps. There's also a triangle barrier overlap by T17 Club
  12. 12 Scope 01:53
    I think the barriers experiment was interesting, however it appears too difficult a problem to solve. Remove the barriers and the tec-pro from the track
  13. 13 Sibling 01:57
    can you create a separate repo that is a sibling to this one and implement the same silverstone track but in a traditional 3D look?
  14. 14 Fix 02:01
    it does not seem to be running, can you try again?
  15. 15 Package 02:12
    for the voxel and 3D versions of trail-brakers that was created, can you package them up in a single git repo (perhaps merge them into the a trail-brakers repo) and create a landing page showcase both projects?
  16. 16 Ship 02:21
    can you deploy this to vercel after adding a banner at the top of the landing page that this was made with grok 4.5?
